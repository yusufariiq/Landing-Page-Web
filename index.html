LOGITECH K380

Minimalist multidevice bluetooth keyboard for computers, tablets and phones.

OWN. YOUR SPACE.

Make every space minimalist, modern and versatile with the K380 Multi-Device. Ultra-slim keyboards with a futuristic design are perfect for typing on computers, smartphones, tablets and many more. This keyboard is the ideal companion for everyday multitasking.

MAIN FEATURES

MINIMALIST

Minimalist, modern and easy to carry. The thin and light K380 Multi-Device Keyboard is equipped with Bluetooth® so you can multitask at home, on the go, or at your favorite coffee shop. Type on your laptop, phone or tablet and control your space wherever you are.

MULTI-DEVICE

Any device, any OS. The K380 Multi-Device connects to any Bluetooth wireless device with external keyboard support so you can work seamlessly with Windows®, macOS, iPadOS, Chrome OS™, Android™, iOS, and even Apple TV.

EASY-SWITCH

Connect up to three devices, even with different operating systems, and switch between them with a tap. So you can type, switch devices, and keep typing.

COZY DESIGN

All buttons are in tight design. A very small footprint allows you to place the mouse closer for greater reach, more comfort and better posture.

KEYBOARD INSTALLATION

STEP 1

Pull the Pull-Tab

Pull the pull-tab from the back of the keyboard and the keyboard will automatically turn on.

STEP 3

Pairing Mode

Press the Channel 1 button for about 3 seconds to enter pairing mode. The LED above the Channel button will start flashing.

STEP 3

Choose your device

Open Bluetooth® preferences on your computer, phone or tablet. Add a new peripheral by selecting “K380 Keyboard” from the list of devices. Your keyboard will ask for a PIN code to complete the connection process. On the keyboard, type the code that appears on the screen, then press the Return or Enter key.

MULTI-DEVICE INSTALLATION

STEP 1

Pair the keyboard with the device

Press the Easy-Switch button for 3 seconds - Channel 2. When the LED starts flashing, the second channel is ready for pairing via Bluetooth connection.

STEP 3

Switch between devices

Briefly press channel 1, 2 or 3 to toggle typing between your devices.

STEP 3

Choose a specific OS Layout

To switch to another layout, press for 3 seconds:

FN and P keys for Windows/Android

FN and O keys for Mac

FN and I keys for iOS

The LED on the associated channel button will light up to indicate the OS was successfully replaced.
